## Key Insights
- **KPI Analysis**: Tracked **Total Sales, Cost, Profit, and Profit Margin** across products, regions, and sales teams to evaluate overall business performance  
- **Revenue Trend Analysis**: Identified a **revenue peak in 2024** followed by a **decline in 2025 with high monthly volatility**, enabling early strategic intervention  
- **Regional Concentration Risk**: Uncovered **~68% revenue concentration in one region** and **~65% in a single store**, highlighting critical business risk and need for diversification  
- **Geographic Insights**: Visualized **regional sales distribution** to identify high-performing and underperforming markets  
- **Top-N Analysis**: Ranked **top-performing products and sales teams** to prioritize high-impact business areas  
- **Forecasting**: Delivered **2026 revenue projections** using time-series analysis for forward-looking decision making  

## Tasks Performed
- **Data Extraction (MySQL)**: Queried raw sales data using **joins, CTEs, subqueries, and aggregations** to build a structured dataset  
- **Data Cleaning (Excel)**: Handled **missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies** to ensure data accuracy  
- **Data Transformation**: Converted raw transactional data into an **analysis-ready format** for reporting and visualization  
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Analyzed **trends, profitability, and regional dependencies** to uncover key insights  
- **KPI & Profitability Analysis**: Computed **cost, profit, and profit margins** across multiple dimensions to identify performance gaps  
- **Dashboard Development (Tableau)**: Built **interactive dashboards** with time-series charts, geographic maps, and Top-N filters  
- **Stakeholder Reporting**: Delivered **actionable insights and strategic recommendations** for data-driven decision making  

## Tools Used
- **MySQL**: Advanced SQL (**joins, CTEs, aggregations**) for data extraction  
- **MS Excel**: Data cleaning, **Pivot Tables, Slicers, and time-based analysis**  
- **Tableau**: Interactive dashboards, **geographic maps, trend analysis, and forecasting**  
- **Data Analytics & KPI Tracking**: Business performance monitoring using key metrics  
- **Data Visualization**: Time-series charts, maps, filters, and Top-N analysis
