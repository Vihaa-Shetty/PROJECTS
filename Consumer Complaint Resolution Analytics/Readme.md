## Key Insights
- **Complaint Volume Trends**: Monitored complaint volume over time and identified **trend spikes and seasonal patterns**, surfacing service gaps across banking and credit card products  
- **KPI Performance**: Tracked **timely response rate (97.8%)**, **dispute rate (18.7%)**, and **resolution efficiency (82.8%)** benchmarked against BFSI industry standards  
- **High Risk Products**: Identified **credit cards and banking services** as top complaint-generating categories requiring priority intervention  
- **Channel Analysis**: Uncovered **web and referral channels** as dominant complaint sources, enabling targeted resolution strategies  
- **Dispute Rate Insights**: Analyzed dispute patterns across segments to identify **root causes** and recommend reduction strategies  
- **Resolution Efficiency**: Evaluated resolution workflows to identify **bottlenecks** and optimize end-to-end processes  

## Tasks Performed
- **Data Collection**: Gathered **75,000+ financial complaint records** across products, channels, and customer segments  
- **Data Cleaning (Python)**: Used **Pandas and NumPy** to handle missing values, remove inconsistencies, and ensure data accuracy  
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**: Performed in-depth analysis using **Matplotlib and Seaborn** to identify distributions, outliers, and complaint patterns  
- **KPI Design and Tracking**: Designed and monitored **timely response rate, dispute rate, and resolution efficiency** aligned with BFSI benchmarks  
- **Product and Channel Analysis**: Segmented complaints by **product category and channel (web vs referral)** to identify high-risk areas  
- **Trend Analysis**: Analyzed time-based complaint trends to detect **seasonal spikes and recurring patterns**  
- **Dashboard Development (Tableau)**: Built **interactive dashboards** with KPI scorecards, trend charts, product breakdowns, and filters  
- **Stakeholder Reporting**: Delivered **actionable insights and recommendations** to reduce disputes and improve resolution efficiency  

## Tools Used
- **Python (EDA)**: **Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn** for data cleaning, validation, and analysis  
- **Tableau**: Interactive dashboards, **trend visualization, KPI tracking, and segmentation analysis**  
- **Data Analytics & KPI Tracking**: Monitoring operational performance using key metrics  
- **Data Visualization**: Charts, distributions, and dashboards for actionable business insights
